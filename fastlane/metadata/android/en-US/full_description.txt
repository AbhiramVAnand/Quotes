<b>Quotes Status Creator</b>

Quotes Status Creator lets you share quotations as status images on social media – Quotes Status Creator App

    ☆ Lots of inspirational quotes that can be shared on social media as a high definition images
    ☆ Quotes Status Creation without any Image Editing whatsoever — in the same user-friendly UI
    ☆ Browse through Best Quotes → Get Inspired → Share as Status and Spread Positivity

<b>⚙How it Works</b>

Quotes Status Creator App presents you with Famous Quotations. Browse through them, get inspired, share status via social media and spread positivity!

<b>🚀Features</b>

    • <b>🔖Favorite Quotes</b> — Add quotes to favorites
    • <b>🔀Share Quotes</b> — Share quotes as a high definition images on social media
    • <b>🖼️Custom Backgrounds</b> — Personalized quote statuses with custom background images, solid background colors or images from gallery
    • <b>🎨Custom Card Colors</b> — Personalized quote statuses with custom card colors
    • <b>🔠Custom Fonts</b> — Personalize quotes statuses with custom fonts
    • <b>🔡Custom Font Colors</b> — Personalize quotes statuses with custom font colors
    • <b>✍🏿Custom Quotes</b> — Add your own quotes
    • <b>🔔Daily Notification</b> — Get notified with your daily dose of motivation
    • <b>🔁Multiple Sharing Option</b> — Multiple sharing options</b> — Share as image, as text (copy as text to the clipboard) and save to gallery
    • <b>🚪App Shortcuts</b> — App shortcuts for easy access
    • <b>🔍Search</b> — Search for quotes to find ones you like
    • <b>🏷️Tags</b> — Search for quotes using tags
    • <b>📱Home Screen Widget</b> — There is an auto-updating quote widget
    • <b>🕶Dark Mode</b> — It fits your soul
    • <b>👨‍💻Completely libre software</b> — Copylefted libre software, licensed GPLv3+
    • <b>🚫Completely Ad Free</b> — No ads anywhere
