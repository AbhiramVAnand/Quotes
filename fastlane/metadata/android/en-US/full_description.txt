Quotes Status Creator lets you share quotations as status images on social media – Quotes Status Creator App

    ☆ Huge Collection of Inspirational Quotes that can be shared on Social Media Platforms as a high definition image
    ☆ Quotes Status Creation without any Image Editing whatsoever - that too in User-Friendly UI
    ☆ Browse through Best Quotes → Get Inspired → Share as Status and Spread Positivity

<b>⚙How it Works</b>

Quotes Status Creator App presents you with Famous Quotations. Browse through them, get inspired, share status via social media and spread positivity!

<b>🚀Features</b>

    • <b>❤Favourite Quotes</b> – Add Quotes to Favourites
    • <b>😎Share Quotes</b> – Share Quotes as a High Definition Image to Social Media
    • <b>📷Custom Backgrounds</b> – Personalize Quotes Statuses with Custom Background Images, Solid Background Colors or Images from Gallery
    • <b>🎨Custom Card Colors</b> – Personalize Quotes Statuses with Custom Card Colors
    • <b>🔠Custom Fonts</b> – Personalize Quotes Statuses with Custom Fonts
    • <b>🔠Custom Font Colors</b> – Personalize Quotes Statuses with Custom Font Colors
    • <b>🆕Custom Quotes</b> – Add your own Quotes
    • <b>🔔Daily Notification</b> – Get notified with your daily dose of motivation
    • <b>🔢Multiple Sharing Option</b> – Multiple Sharing Options - Share as Image, Share as Text (copy as text to the clipboard) and Save to Gallery
    • <b>🚪App Shortcuts</b> – Quotes Status Creator includes App Shortcuts for Easy Access
• <b>🔍Search</b> – Search for Quotes to find Quotes you like
    • <b>🏠Home Screen Widget</b> – Quotes Status Creator App includes Auto-Updating Quotes Widget
    • <b>🕶Dark Mode</b> – Quotes Status Creator App includes Dark Mode
    • <b>👨‍💻Completely Open Source</b> – Quotes Status Creator App is Completely Open Source
    • <b>🎉Completely Ad Free</b> – Quotes Status Creator App is Completely Free from Ads
